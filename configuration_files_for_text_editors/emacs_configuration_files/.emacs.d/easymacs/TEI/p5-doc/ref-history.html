TEI P5: Guidelines for Electronic Text Encoding and Interchange

<history>

history groups elements describing the full history of a manuscript or manuscript part.
Attributes: Global attributes only
Declaration
element history
{
   att.global.attributes,
   ( model.pLike+ | ( origin?, provenance*, acquisition? ) )
}
Example
 <history>
  <origin>
   <p>Written in Durham during the mid twelfth century.</p>
  </origin>
  <provenance>
   <p>Recorded in two medieval catalogues of the books belonging to Durham Priory, made in 1391 and 1405.</p>
   <p>Given to W. Olleyf by William Ebchester, Prior (1446-56) and later belonged to Henry Dalton, Prior of Holy Island (Lindisfarne) according to inscriptions on ff. 4v and 5.</p>
  </provenance>
  <acquisition>
   <p>Presented to Trinity College in 1738 by Thomas Gale and his son Roger.</p>
  </acquisition>
 </history>
