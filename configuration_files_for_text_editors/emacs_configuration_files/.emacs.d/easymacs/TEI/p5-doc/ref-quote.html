TEI P5: Guidelines for Electronic Text Encoding and Interchange

<quote>

quote(quotation) contains a phrase or passage attributed by the narrator or author to some agency external to the text.
Attributes: [att.msExcerpt ]
Classmodel.qLike model.quoteLike att.msExcerpt
Declaration
element quote
{
   att.global.attributes,
   att.msExcerpt.attributes,
   macro.specialPara
}
Example
Lexicography has shown little sign of being affected by the work of followers of J.R. Firth, probably best summarized in his slogan,  <quote>You shall know a word by the company it keeps</quote>
 <ref>(Firth, 1957)</ref>
Note

If a bibliographic citation is supplied for the source of a quotation, the two may be grouped using the <cit> element.
